Our Story

Clarity, redefined.

eNVision was born from a simple belief that everyone deserves to see the world in perfect clarity. What started as a small optical studio has grown into a destination for modern eyewear, crafted with care and designed for life.

Our Origin

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Terang Cerah Merona, eNVision began as a small workshop with a big dream. Our founders, two optometry graduates, noticed a gap in the market. Eyewear was either too clinical or too trendy, with little attention to how it actually felt to wear.

They set out to create frames that balanced precision optics with everyday comfort. Every frame is tested for fit, weight, and durability before it reaches your hands.

Our Philosophy

We believe eyewear is more than a prescription, it is an extension of how you express yourself and how you experience the world.

Every frame we design follows three principles: comfort that lasts all day, clarity that meets medical standards, and style that feels naturally yours.
